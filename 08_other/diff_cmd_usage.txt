diff -y left-file right-file

2 column side-by-side comparison, where | shows that the line has been modified

In the standard notation, it shows the modified lines separated by a  ---

4d3, 5a5, 4c4 etc are all based on the syntax of the ed-commands
left number (target line on the left file)
right number (source of information for the change).

Can be single lines or ranges of lines
eg. 1,3c1,3
